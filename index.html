Our Team

We're a family owned and operated business that was started right here in Dallas to bring you top-notch DJ and Events services.

Oren Pajela

Founder & DJ

Oren passion and curiosity for all things music started when he learned how to play the violin when he was 5 years old. He taught himself many different instruments over the years, including the guitar, piano, and ukulele until eventually he got his hands on his first DJ controller and started experimenting with digital music production.

Oren graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas in 2017 with a B.S. in Business Administration and is also pursuing a Master's degree in Innovation and Entrepreneurship at UTD. He uses his professional and educational experience to ensure that he brings the best DJ/MC services to your event so that you and your guests can build memories that last. He enjoys spending time with his lovely best friend and wife, Camille, and their dog, Wyatt.

Oren loves all types of music, from pop music to classical music, from EDM to heavy metal, and has a vast knowledge of musical forms and styles. Some of his favorite artists include Daft Punk, Foo Fighters, Jacob Collier, J. Cole, The Beatles, and more.

Camille Pajela

Founder & Event Coordinator

Camille is one of music's biggest fans and is able to combine her passions of music and event coordination/planning at RLC Events. She loves singing out loud at home, and also learned how to play the Ukulele all by herself. She brings her own knowledge of music to the team and knows all the best songs to be played at any event at any time.

Camille brings her experience as a planner, marketer, and coordinator to RLC Events. She graduated from the University of Texas at Dallas in 2018 with a B.S. in Marketing, and has become a vital part of Ericsson's Marketing and Communication Team. She loves golfing, traveling, and spending time with her best friend and husband, Oren, and their grumpy dog, Wyatt. She is a big fan of video games, particularly Call of Duty: Warzone.

Camille is a big fan of all music, but is particularly fond of pop music and country music. Some of her favorite artists include Taylor Swift, Luke Bryan, Kygo, Thomas Rhett, and more. She is also familiar with the current times and knows almost all of the Tik Tok songs and dances.

Cameron Mendoza

Sales & Equipment

Cameron listened to a bit of music growing up but really fell in love with it when he began to play guitar at age 13. He became a self-taught multi-instrumentalist who became very involved in church worship during his later high school years. It was here that he started his involvement with the logistics and planning behind creating musical experiences.

His college career placed him in various event planning and coordinating roles, starting off as a stagehand for Baylor University's Vertical college ministries and eventually working as a worship leader and interim youth minister.

Cameron graduated from Baylor's Truett Seminary and has spent his time back in DFW diving into music theory, learning his instruments in a more traditional manner, and working on certifications to give accreditation to the work he has done in event coordination, organization, and analysis.
